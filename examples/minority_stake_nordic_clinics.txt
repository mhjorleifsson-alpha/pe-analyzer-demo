TRANSACTION OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Nordic Specialist Clinics AB ("NSC" or the "Company") is a Swedish private
healthcare services group operating a network of 24 specialist outpatient
clinics across Sweden, Norway, and Finland. The majority shareholder (Norvik
Family Office, 68%) is seeking to bring in a minority financial partner to
support the Company's continued organic growth and selective bolt-on acquisition
programme. The transaction involves the issuance of new equity representing
20%–30% of the enlarged share capital; no existing shares are being sold.

The Company is not seeking a change of control. Governance rights — including
one board seat and standard minority protections — will be offered to the
incoming investor.

GROWTH STRATEGY
================================================================================

1. ORGANIC CLINIC ROLLOUT
   Management has identified 6 target cities for new clinic openings over
   FY2026–FY2027, requiring SEK 20M–SEK 30M per clinic. Site selection is
   driven by population density, proximity to corporate HQ clusters (for
   insurance referrals), and limited specialist capacity in the local market.

2. BOLT-ON ACQUISITIONS
   The fragmented Nordic specialist outpatient market offers consolidation
   opportunities. NSC has a pipeline of 5 targets with combined revenue of
   SEK ~320M, at valuation multiples of 7x–9x EBITDA (below NSC's own
   multiple, creating immediate accretion).

3. DIGITAL PATIENT EXPERIENCE
   NSC is investing SEK 35M over FY2026–FY2027 in a proprietary patient app
   and online booking/referral platform. Digital bookings currently represent
   29% of volume; target is 60% by end of FY2027.

4. INSURANCE PARTNERSHIP DEEPENING
   New preferred provider agreements with two additional Scandinavian insurers
   (combined covered lives: ~1.4M) are expected to be signed in H1 2026,
   adding an estimated SEK 80M–SEK 110M of annualised referral revenue by 2027.

================================================================================
INVESTMENT HIGHLIGHTS
================================================================================

1. DEFENSIVE, GROWING REVENUE MIX
   58% of revenue from multi-year corporate insurance contracts with low
   churn. Healthcare demand is structurally growing due to ageing demographics
   and increasing corporate wellness spend across Scandinavia.

2. HIGH-QUALITY EARNINGS
   20% EBITDA margins with 63% FCF conversion reflect a capital-light clinic
   model (leased premises), experienced clinical teams under long-term employment
   agreements, and a low bad-debt rate (<0.3% of revenue).

3. PLATFORM FOR REGIONAL CONSOLIDATION
   NSC is the third-largest private specialist outpatient operator in the Nordic
   region by revenue. The top two operators (Capio and Aleris) are 4x–5x the
   size of NSC, leaving significant room for organic and acquisitive growth
   before reaching platform maturity.

4. STRONG MANAGEMENT TEAM
   CEO Erik Lindström (15 years in Nordic private healthcare) and CFO Anna
   Bergström (former VP Finance, Ramsay Générale de Santé) have been with the
   business since 2016 and 2019 respectively.
