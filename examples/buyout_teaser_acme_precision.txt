================================================================================
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

Acme Precision Components GmbH ("Acme" or the "Company") is a German precision
engineering manufacturer headquartered in Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg. Founded
in 1987, Acme produces high-tolerance hydraulic and pneumatic components for
the automotive Tier 1, industrial machinery, and medical devices sectors.

The Company's controlling shareholders (the founding Brenner family, holding
73%) are seeking to retire and are inviting PE sponsors to acquire a majority
stake or full control of the business via a leveraged buyout transaction.

================================================================================
COMPANY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Company:        Acme Precision Components GmbH
Headquarters:   Stuttgart, Germany
Founded:        1987
Industry:       Precision Engineering / Industrial Manufacturing
Sector:         Automotive & Industrial Components
Employees:      412 (as of December 2025)
Facilities:     3 manufacturing sites (Stuttgart, Ulm, Wrocław, Poland)

Key customers include Bosch, ZF Friedrichshafen, and Siemens Energy. No single
customer accounts for more than 18% of revenue. The customer base is highly
sticky: average customer tenure exceeds 11 years and the Company holds
multi-year supply agreements with its top 5 customers.

================================================================================
INVESTMENT HIGHLIGHTS
================================================================================

1. RECESSION-RESILIENT REVENUE BASE
   Long-term supply contracts with Tier 1 automotive OEMs and regulated medical
   device manufacturers provide high revenue visibility. 78% of FY2025 revenue
   was under multi-year contracts.

2. HIGH BARRIERS TO ENTRY
   Customer qualification processes in automotive and medical devices take 18–36
   months. Acme has ISO 9001:2015, IATF 16949, and ISO 13485 certifications.

3. STRONG CASH CONVERSION
   EBITDA-to-FCF conversion of ~70% reflects low maintenance capex requirements
   and disciplined working capital management.

4. MARGIN EXPANSION OPPORTUNITY
   Integration of the Wrocław facility (opened 2023) is expected to deliver
   €1.8M–€2.4M of incremental EBITDA by FY2027 as utilisation ramps.

5. FRAGMENTED MARKET WITH BOLT-ON POTENTIAL
   The European precision components sector is highly fragmented. Management
   has identified three potential bolt-on targets with combined revenue of
   approximately €40M.
