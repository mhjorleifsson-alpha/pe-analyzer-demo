OPPORTUNITY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

SynthAI Analytics Ltd. ("SynthAI" or the "Company") is a UK-based B2B SaaS
platform providing AI-powered supply chain intelligence to mid-market and
enterprise manufacturers in Europe and North America. The Company is seeking a
Series B growth equity investment of £18M–£22M to accelerate its go-to-market
expansion into the DACH region and fund a 40-person headcount increase across
product and sales.

The round values the Company at approximately £95M–£110M pre-money.
